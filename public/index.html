Reviews & Perspectives
Journal: Expert Review of Clinical Pharmacology

Limits of Inter-Hospital Model Transfer in Delirium Detection

External Validation
Journal: J Gerontol A Biol Sci Med Sci

A multi-institution study deployed an open-source NLP model to detect delirium features from EHR notes, achieving feasible but variable performance across seven health systems. The project established a shared ontology, temporal conventions, and site-specific preprocessing to improve portability and consistency.
